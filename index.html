Active Fantasy Sports

Active Fantasy Sports is a new way to play fantasy sports. In most ways, it's just like regular online fantasy sports. However, there's an important twist: in Active Fantasy Sports, you and your friends (or enemies) are assigned weekly physical activity goals. League privleges (e.g., waiver wire priority) are dependent on team owners' weekly physical activity levels.The league commissioner can customize weekly goals, privleges awarded, as well as concequnces for not meeting goals using our league-creation tool. The goal of Active Fantasy Sports is to promote physical well-being..

What is Active Fantasy Sports?

Active Fantasy Sports helps motivate you to work out by tethering your Fitbit activity to privileges and punishments in your season-long fantasy sports league.

More Info

How is physical activity measured?

For the purposes of Active Fantasy Sports, physical activity refers to number of steps taken in a given day, as measrued by a Fitbit fitness tracker (any of Fitbit's numerous fitness tracking solutions will work). When you sync your Fitbit with your computer or mobile device, Active Fantasy Sports will automatically synchronize with your Fitbit account.

Will Active Fantasy Sports actually motivate me to exercise?

Preliminary research done by Arlen Moller, PhD suggests that Active Fantasy Sports does indeed promote exercise! Dr. Moller is actively researching the benefits of Active Fantasy Sports. If you'd like stay up-to-date with the research findings about Active Fantasy Sports, you can join our electronic waiting list.
